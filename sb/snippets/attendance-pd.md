Lectures are presented live, simulcast, and recorded for later viewing. Please join us according to your enrollment and availability:

- ABM Fellowship students are *required* to attend in person
- On-campus graduate students are *expected* to attend in person
- Distance students are *asked* to join live via Zoom when their schedule allows
- All lectures are recorded and available for asynchronous viewing

ABM Fellowship students must miss no more than 2 lectures and earn a B or better to receive their fellowship funding.
